# Inbox Overview

The Inbox is where your team handles every incoming client message — email, SMS, and WhatsApp — in one shared workspace. Instead of messages scattered across personal phones and separate email accounts, everything arrives in Interline where the whole team can see it, reply, hand off, and keep track of what's been handled.

## What you can do in the Inbox

- Read and reply to messages across **all three channels** from one screen.
- Work out of **shared team mailboxes** as well as your own **personal** assigned messages.
- View a **unified inbox** of everything, or **separate inboxes** per channel.
- Organize conversations with **status**, **priority**, and custom **tags**.
- Collaborate with teammates using **internal comments** and **@mentions**.
- Reply faster with **canned messages** and **attachments** from a shared library.
- See full **contact details** alongside every conversation.
- Set up **auto-responders** for after-hours or out-of-office replies.

## Key concepts at a glance

A **conversation** (or thread) is the running history of messages with one contact on one channel. A **mailbox** is a collection of conversations — either a shared team mailbox or your personal one. **Status**, **priority**, and **tags** are the three ways you label conversations so the right ones rise to the top. **Assigning** a conversation makes one person responsible for it; **closing** it signals the work is done and removes it from the active queue.
