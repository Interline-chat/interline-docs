# Keywords Overview

!!! warning "Skeleton — content coming next"
    This section is an outline. The Inbox guide is the finished template; Keywords will be written out to match.

Keywords automatically reply when a client texts a specific word. Text `HOURS` and get your opening times back; text `SIGN ME UP` and get added to a contact list. This overview will explain how keyword triggers work and walk through building your first one.
