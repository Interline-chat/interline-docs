# Broadcast Overview

!!! warning "Skeleton — content coming next"
    This section is an outline. The Inbox guide is the finished template; Broadcast will be written out to match.

Broadcast lets you send a single SMS or WhatsApp message to a whole list of contacts at once — order reminders, holiday greetings, promotions, and more. This overview will explain when to use Broadcast and walk through composing and sending your first campaign.

## Planned pages

The Broadcast guide will be split into the following pages:

### Composing a campaign
- The compose tool: writing your message
- Adding text, images, and attachments
- Personalizing with **{variables}**
- Live preview of the message and recipient count

### Choosing your audience
- Selecting one or more contact lists to send to
- Excluding a list
- Filtering to contacts in specific groups or with specific tags

### Choosing how it sends
- Sending over a specific channel (SMS or WhatsApp)
- Sending over the channel the contact last interacted on
- Message **segments**: how character length and encoding affect SMS count and cost
- "Reply STOP to stop" / opt-out handling

### Scheduling & automation
- Scheduling a campaign for a later date and time
- Recurring messages
- Saving and reusing message **templates** (weekly orders, holiday greetings)

### Before you send
- Sending a **test message** to yourself
- Saving a campaign as a **draft**

### Managing campaigns
- The campaign list: previous and scheduled
- Statuses: sent, completed, scheduled, draft
- Campaign details: recipient count, time sent, and more
